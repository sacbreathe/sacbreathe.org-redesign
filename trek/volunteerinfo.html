Volunteer Information

Join us for three days of fun in Petaluma with your closest volunteer friends and family September 8-10, 2017! We are infusing new afternoon and evening entertainment along with our awards dinner and presentation, themed Saturday night dance and three day bike Trek.

Volunteers contribute heavily to the success of the Breathe Bike Trek. This event would not be possible without such a dedicated team of people.

Included in your Volunteer Package:

  • Camping at the beautiful Petaluma KOA
  • Three days volunteering along the beautiful scenic roads or in-camp
  • A variety of volunteer opportunities available
  • Delicious meals catered by Feast Catering
  • In-camp snacks
  • Evening entertainment
  • FUN!

Registration coming soon.

Most of the volunteer positions require a three-day commitment. Must be 18 years or older or accompanied by an adult to volunteer.
